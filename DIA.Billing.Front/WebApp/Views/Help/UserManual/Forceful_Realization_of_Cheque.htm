Forceful Realization of Cheque

 

  1. Selects the relevant UI.
  2. Key-in full cheque number (Cheque No, Bank code and branch code).
  3. Upon keying-in, relevant Receipt Details, Cheque Amount, Cheque Date, Bank Name and Branch name will be loaded.

 

Special Note:

  • If there are already cancelled receipts, those will be hi-lighted shown. Those will not be submitted.

 

  1. Click on ‘Submit’ button.
